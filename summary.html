Summary

Project HEAR (Haptic Environmental Audio Receiver) is a project that aims to assist individuals who experience a lack of emergency response due to their difficulty of hearing. The implementation of Project HEAR in schools will help assist not only in accommodating deaf students, but also help deaf students have a more accessible and cost-efficient form of hearing aids and cochlear implants.

Pre-Order Form Summary

The pre-order form for Project HEAR allows customers to select the variant of the product they want to purchase, specify the quantity, choose a pickup location, and provide their address. The form also includes a checkbox for agreeing to the terms and conditions. Upon submission, the form validates the input and stores the order details in a cookie for later retrieval.

Customer Name

The customer's name is collected through a text input field in the pre-order form. This information is essential for processing the order and providing personalized service.

Product Variant

Customers can choose between two variants of the Project HEAR product: Standard and Premium. Each variant has a different price point, with the Standard version priced at $99.99 and the Premium version priced at $199.99.

Quantity

Customers can specify the quantity of the product they wish to purchase using a number input field. The minimum quantity allowed is 1, and the default value is set to 1.

Pickup Method

Customers can select their preferred pickup method from a dropdown menu. The options include Delivery and Meetup. If the customer selects Delivery, they will be required to provide their address for the delivery of the product.

Address

The address field is intended for customers who choose the Delivery pickup method. This information is necessary to ensure that the product is delivered to the correct location.

Terms and Conditions

Customers must agree to the terms and conditions by checking a checkbox before they can submit their order. This is a standard practice to ensure that customers are aware of the policies and agreements related to their purchase.
